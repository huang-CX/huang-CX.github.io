Changxin Huang

I am currently a PhD student at Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Liang Lin at Human Cyber Physical Intelligence Integration Lab . Previously, I received my Bachelor degree in Automation in 2015 from South China University of Technology and my Master degree in Information and Communication Engineering in 2021 from Sichuan University. My research interests include machine learning, deep reinforcement learning, and their applications in robotics.
