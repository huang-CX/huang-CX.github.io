Videos

Reward-Adaptive Reinforcement Learning: Dynamic Policy Gradient Optimization for Bipedal Locomotion (T-PAMI 2022)

It's challenging to control non-statically bipedal robots due to complex dynamics and multi-criterion optimization. A scalar reward function may be insufficient for DRL approaches. The proposed reward-adaptive RL method called hybrid and dynamic policy gradient optimization outperforms summed-up-reward approaches and can transfer to physical robots.

Deductive Reinforcement Learning for Visual Autonomous Urban Driving Navigation (TNNLS 2021)

This work presents a new approach called DeRL for vision-based autonomous urban driving navigation. The approach incorporates a deduction reasoner (DR) to predict future transitions and assess the predicted trajectory for more reliable decision-making. Additionally, a semantic encoder module (SEM) is included to extract driving representations from raw images. The proposed DeRL outperforms existing model-free RL approaches on the CARLA benchmark.
